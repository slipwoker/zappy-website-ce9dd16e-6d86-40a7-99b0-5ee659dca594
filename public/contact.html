Common Questions

How does client registration work?

Client registration allows us to match you with exclusive investment opportunities and share detailed property materials tailored to your criteria. Once registered, you'll receive curated listings, market insights, and priority access to new assets before they hit the broader market.

Can I submit just an address?

Absolutely. Address-based requests are welcome and often the fastest way to get specific property information. Simply provide the address through our lookup tool, and we'll deliver comprehensive details including ownership, valuation, leasing status, and investment potential.

Do you handle leasing only?

Yes, we support leasing-only engagements for property owners and investors seeking tenant placement and lease management services. Our leasing team handles everything from marketing and showings to tenant screening and lease execution.

Do you manage assets post-transaction?

Full property management and reporting support is available for clients who need ongoing asset oversight. We provide rent collection, maintenance coordination, financial reporting, and performance analytics to maximize your investment returns and minimize operational headaches.

What types of properties do you specialize in?

We specialize in commercial real estate, multi-family residential, and mixed-use developments. Our team has deep expertise in income-generating properties and can assist with acquisitions, dispositions, and portfolio optimization across all asset classes.

How quickly can I get property information?

Most address lookups and property requests are processed within 24-48 hours. For registered clients with active portfolios, we often provide same-day responses. Complex due diligence requests may require additional time depending on data availability and scope.
